## 🎯 What is West?

West is an AI-powered content copilot that helps Binance Square creators produce high-quality educational crypto content — fast.

**The problem:** Creating consistent, quality crypto content is hard. Research takes time, writing is difficult (especially for beginners), and maintaining presence across multiple platforms is exhausting.

**The solution:** West turns raw market data into publish-ready content for Binance Square and X in minutes.

## ⚙️ How It Works

```
📊 Market Data/News → 🤖 West Processes → 📝 Ready-to-Publish Content
```

1. **Input** — Provide technical data, market news, or analysis points
2. **Process** — West generates platform-optimized content
3. **Output** — Full articles (Binance Square) + threads (X) ready to post
4. **Review & Publish** — Creator reviews, adds personal touch, and publishes

## ✨ Key Features

- 📝 **Article Generator** — Turn data points into ~300-word professional Binance Square articles
- 🧵 **Thread Creator** — Convert articles into engaging 5-7 tweet X threads
- 📊 **Technical Analysis Writer** — Explain RSI, MA, Support/Resistance in simple language
- 🌐 **Multi-Platform** — 1 analysis → content for Binance Square + X simultaneously
- 🎓 **Beginner-Friendly** — No writing experience needed, just provide the data
- 🧠 **Memory & Context** — Remembers your style, preferences, and past interactions
- ⚡ **Write to Earn Accelerator** — Help creators post consistently on Binance Square

## 🔧 Technical Configuration

```

1. Binance Square Article Generator (~300 words, professional, beginner-friendly)
```
